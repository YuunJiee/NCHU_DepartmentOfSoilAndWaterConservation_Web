Introduction

Forming a Highest Area for Soil and Water Conservation Researches

Seeking excellent teachers and student in order to foster talented person. Improving teaching and research in order to promote the academic level and studying atmosphere.

Enhancing Service and Information Provision

Promoting the establishment of soil and water conservation research and information center in order to provide the information and technologies in the field of soil and water conservation.

Increasing Teaching and Researching Facilities

Enlarging the department space in order to improve our teaching and research environment. Increasing the budget in order to by more precision instrument, books and journal.

Enhancing Research Extension and Cooperation

Coordination with the international science and technology promotion, we will recommend our colleague go abroad to do further study. In the mean while we will invite the erudite person joining our department in order to enhance our teachers' lineup. Encouraging teacher to participate the research plan and social service.
